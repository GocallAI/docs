Welcome to the GoCall.ai documentation. Here, you will find all the necessary information to effectively utilize our platform. GoCall.ai provides advanced AI-driven call analysis and automation to enhance your business communications.

## Getting Started


GoCall.ai leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence to analyze and automate your business calls, ensuring enhanced productivity and customer satisfaction. Our platform is designed to seamlessly integrate with your existing systems, providing insightful analytics and actionable data.

### Features

- **Real-Time Call Analysis:** Get immediate insights during calls to improve engagement and decision-making.
- **Automated Summaries:** Receive concise summaries of your calls, highlighting key points and action items.
- **Sentiment Analysis:** Understand the emotional tone of your conversations to better gauge customer satisfaction.
- **Integration with CRM:** Easily sync call data with your CRM for streamlined workflow and data consistency.

## FAQ

### How do I reset my password?

- Go to the login page and click 'Forgot Password.' Follow the instructions to reset your password.

### Can I export call data?

- Yes, you can export call data from the 'Reports' section in your dashboard.

### Is GoCall.ai GDPR compliant?

- Yes, GoCall.ai complies with GDPR regulations. For more information, visit our [Privacy Policy](https://gocall.ai/privacy).
